BEATcancer™

BEATcancer™ tests a patient's tumor biopsy with 30 combinations of 40 drugs at different doses to identify the set of drugs that gives the best response.

CUPid

CUPid identifies the primary sites of cancers of unknown primary (CUP) and identifies targetable mutations.

We sequence every gene and test them on an ensemble of models based on different sets of genes. This approach to machine learning gives us a clearer picture of the unknown cancer type.
